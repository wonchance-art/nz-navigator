Skilled Migrant Category Resident Visa

  • Length of stay

    Live, work and study

    indefinitely

  • Cost

    From

    NZD $6450

  • Age range

    From

    55 years or younger

Income

You can claim:

  • 6 points — if the job is at 3 times the 2025 median wage (currently NZD $105.00 an hour) or more
  • 4 points — if the job is at 2 times the 2025 median wage (currently NZD $70.00 an hour) or more
  • 3 points — if the job is at 1.5 times the 2025 median wage (currently NZD $52.50 an hour) or more.
